Amelie's movie watching journal

18/12/2025

Tomorrow is the next film club meetup!

What will we watch? Probably a Christmas film. Mrs. Miracle 2: Miracle in Manhattan perhaps? It is one of the greatest Christmas movies of all time after all... An all time classic (if you're me).

14/12/2025

Yesterday I went to the cinema and watched Wicked!

I had enjoyed the first one, but i didn't think the music in this one was quite as good...

13/12/2025

Yesterday I stayed in and we watched The Holdovers on Netflix!

It was a really heartfelt christmas film and felt like a real 'Christmas Classic' despite only being released in the past couple of years.

08/12/2025

Yesterday we watched Christmas Under the Northern Lights on Netflix.

I didn't pay much attention to it, which probably hindered the watch, but it was another one of those 'Hallmark' style Christmas movies. Nothing special and contained very little drama.

06/12/2025

Yesterday I rewatched both Amadeus and Ginger Snaps.

Two of my favourite films of all time. I am not particularly keen on rewatching films but I think I could rewatch both of these every day. Very different films which I love for very different reasons.
